Disputes

In the hostile and unfamiliar terrain confronting a distressed company, we provide assistance in various situations including underperformance compared with forecasts, cash difficulties, business reorganisation, debt renegotiation, launch of amicable or legal collective proceedings, distressed M&A and many more.
We work with all stakeholders involved in a distressed situation, be they company management, shareholders, creditors, receivers, potential buyers or others. We combine our economic, financial, valuation, strategic, and accounting expertise to provide essential, bespoke, incisive support to take rapid and often far-reaching decisions with major implications for the business.
Our proven expertise in crisis environments, our knowledge of key players and our technical analyses on ensure that optimal solutions are implemented in distressed situations.
